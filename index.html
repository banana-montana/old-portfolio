    FOD 10 Year Poster

    Illustration

    10 Year Celebratory Magazine Poster for Friends of Design.

    Friends of Design, a creative college in Cape Town, celebrated their 10 year anniversary and asked me to custom illustrate a poster for their magazine they were creating in honour of their birthday. The specs were for it to be in black, white and this specific shade of green in a flat line art style.

    Godchecker Redesign

    Web design & UX/UI

    This is a redesign project for Godchecker - a quirky and humorous website that needed a face lift to show its awesome content. I was required to redesign the 5 main pages of the site as a college project. My focus was on the UI as much as the functionality of the site. I wanted to keep a bright, simple colour scheme and keep it's fun factor in the design.

    Fruitea Tea

    branding & logo

    Fruitea is a fun fruit flavoured tea brand that was designed to appeal to a younger market. Tea branding and packaging is often dated, cluttered and hard to relate to, but not this one! Fruitea is an organic solution to other drinks and allows for a fresher, simpler and lighter approach to tea that can be enjoyed by everyone.

    London Donut Co.

    Web design & branding

    The London Donut Co. is a small pop-up donut store in Cape Town based on British style. This site was created as a college project for one of our first responsive web sites.

    The Donut Co. web site is straight forward, bright and functional. Their signature donut pattern is used as a common element throughout the site, alongside their red and blue colours, to maintain a sense of unity and vibrancy.

    Pantone website

    Web design & UX/UI

    Pantone is a huge symbol in the design community. With that being said, you'd expect them to have a smashing web site. I felt it needed a remodelling - I cleaned up the UI and allowed the colour to do all of the communicating by giving the site's interface a white and light grey colour scheme.

    The original site has many menu's within menu's for all the information that is necessary to convey so I chose to create a side navigation menu to allow for easy access to those links.

    Manhattan Sweets

    Packaging

    This is a recreation of Manhatthan marshmallow’s packaging. I decided to redesign it as I found the original too cluttered and busy. I changed it from a plastic packet to a recyclable box and gave it a minimalist design and simplified colour palette (along with an adorable yeti monster who likes to eat marshmallows).

    Night of 1000 Drawings

    Marketing campaign

    1000 Drawings is a charity event in Cape Town. It's an event where a group of people come together, doodle beautiful drawings and the doodles get auctioned off with the proceeds going to selected local charities.

    This is what my team and I created for 2017's campaign. We chose to go with a simple black and white theme that featured hand-drawn doodles. We photographed various hands and let the hands do the talking since they would be producing the doodles.

    Generation Y

    magazine & editorial

    Generation Y is a quarterly new age design magazine for the young milenials, the people of the digital age. It is a design magazine that aims to inspire and create desire and love for graphic design and art by interviewing creatives and artists who have tips and tricks to part with. It boasts a simple colour scheme that changes with each edition.

    Color Factory Co.

    Experimental web design

    The Color Factory is a unique mueseum in San Fransisco that celebrates the beauty of color. It's an art exhibit that is one of a kind. Each color has its own room inside this exhibit that contains inventive and original experiences.

    The web site needed to reflect this vibrant and fun side in an interactive way.
