    Let's build something great together

    I’m Lea - a graphic designer, illustrator, web developer and professional cat lady from Cape Town in sunny South Africa. I use visual storytelling to transform complex information and ideas into powerful connections between people and your brand. I write code for the web and favour semantically structured markup, accessibility and well designed CSS. When I am not designing or coding mastermind projects, I am tending to my dying plants and my overly affectionate four cats. A few note-worthy skills I have:

    PRINT

    illustration, poster design, invitations, editorial design, book covers and layouts, greeting cards, packaging, textile prints

    DESIGN

    logo design, branding, typography, photo manipulation, animation, cinematography, marketing & social media content

    WEB

    UX/UI, interface design, HTML, CSS/SCSS, JavaScript & jQuery, WordPress, Vue.js, some other bullshit placed here cherries
